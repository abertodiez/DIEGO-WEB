OHIO
Due to its design, the structure can be joined to create more complex structures; thanks the repeat of a same module. It produces defined, different and independent zones which create several corridors and areas where the privacy is one of the most important element between the different spaces. The inside space of each module, is conceived as a functional space, which have a furniture fixed, and a furniture which can be changed; as tables, chairs..on depends of the different needs of the guests. The structure is formed by lacquered aluminium and polyurethane rubber for the bed. The interior pavement can be realized in nowood or the profiles with a polyurethane rubber as a matress which enable the floor like a rest zone.

PARASITE
The project is composed of a cabin which is overhead the buiding. This cabin uses the installations of the lower building and is fixed to the facade with two huge steel beams. Through a steel stairs you can go to the two floors of the new space, and walk through the gangway which surrondes all volume. The base is realized in wooden slats, and the enclosures are maked in tempered glass and metal sheet.
